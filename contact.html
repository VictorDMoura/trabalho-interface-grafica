Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take reservations?

Yes, we recommend making reservations, especially for dinner and weekends. You can make a reservation by phone or through our online reservation system.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we offer catering for special events and private parties. Please contact us at least one week in advance to discuss menu options and availability.

Is there parking available?

We have a small parking lot behind the restaurant, and there is also street parking available. On weekends, we offer complimentary valet service.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we can accommodate most dietary restrictions and allergies. Please inform your server of any special requirements, and our chef will be happy to prepare suitable options.
