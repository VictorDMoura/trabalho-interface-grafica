Our Story

The history of our family restaurant and the passion behind our cuisine

A Family Tradition

Famiglia Cucina was founded in 1978 by Antonio and Maria Rossi, who immigrated to America with a dream of sharing their beloved family recipes with their new community. What began as a small trattoria has grown into a beloved local institution, while still maintaining the warmth and authenticity that made it special from day one.

For over four decades, we've been serving authentic Italian cuisine made with love, passion, and the finest ingredients. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, preserving the traditional flavors of Italy while incorporating fresh, local produce.

Our Journey

1978

The Beginning

Antonio and Maria Rossi open the doors of Famiglia Cucina in a small storefront with just 8 tables. Their homemade pasta and traditional recipes quickly gain popularity in the neighborhood.

1985

Expansion

After years of success, the restaurant expands to the adjacent building, doubling its capacity and adding a dedicated bar area featuring Italian wines and spirits.

1992

Second Generation

Antonio and Maria's children, Marco and Sofia, join the family business, bringing fresh ideas while maintaining the restaurant's authentic roots and traditions.

2005

Renovation

The restaurant undergoes a major renovation, updating the interior while carefully preserving its warm, family atmosphere and traditional charm.

Today

Continuing the Legacy

Now run by the second and third generations of the Rossi family, Famiglia Cucina continues to honor its heritage while embracing modern culinary techniques and sustainable practices.
